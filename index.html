WHO WE ARE

We’re the Treblemakers, an award-winning co-ed a cappella group at Carnegie Mellon. Founded in 2010, we’ve quickly grown into a musical force to be reckoned with. We host CMU’s Almost Midnight A Cappella Concert every spring and regularly compete in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella where we recently won First Place and Outstanding Choreography at quarterfinals. We pride ourselves on being one of the most fun ­loving groups on campus and we push ourselves to be the best we can be.

At heart, we’re a close knit group of friends who love to share our passion with each other and our community. Treblemakers is a group for all kinds of singers and all styles of music, and we work together to bring our visions to life. We love new friends, so if you’re interested in singing with us, want us to perform for you, or have any questions, just let us know. We’d love to make Treble with you!

AUDITIONS

Auditions for the 2017-18 school year will be held from Thursday, September 7th through Saturday, September 9th. Sign up at the link below, or at the Activities Fair on Wednesday, September 6th on the CFA Lawn from 4:30-6:30.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Please prepare a verse and a chorus from a song that shows off your voice. We’ll take you through a vocal range exercise and there will be an optional sight reading component. We’re looking for all kinds of talent; so if you beatbox, arrange, choreograph, or have leadership experience, be sure to tell us about it. Send us an email if you have any questions. Relax, we can’t wait to meet you!

CALLBACKS

After auditioning, make sure to check your email on Saturday night to see if you've recieved a callback. They will be held from 10AM to 12PM on Sunday, September 10th in Wean 5316. Congratulations!
