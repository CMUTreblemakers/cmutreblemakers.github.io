CMU TREBLEMAKERS

Competitive Co-ed A Cappella

We are pretty great.



We’re the Treblemakers, an award-winning co-ed a cappella group at Carnegie Mellon. Founded in 2010, we’ve quickly grown into a musical force to be reckoned with. We host CMU’s Almost Midnight A Cappella Concert every spring and regularly compete in the International Competition of Collegiate A Cappella. We pride ourselves on being one of the most fun ­loving groups on campus and we push ourselves to be the best we can be.

At heart, we’re a close knit group of friends who love to share our passion with each other and our community. Treblemakers is a group for all kinds of singers and all styles of music, and we work together to bring our visions to life. We love new friends, so if you’re interested in singing with us, want us to perform for you, or have any questions, just let us know. We’d love to make treble with you!

You are pretty great.

And that means that you should come audition for the Treblemakers! Auditions will be from September 8th to September 11th. You’ll need to prepare a short selection of a solo, and we’ll take you through some range, rhythm, and sight reading exercises. We’re looking for all kinds of talent; so if you beatbox, arrange, choreograph, or have leadership experience, be sure to tell us about it. Send us an email if you have any questions. You’ll be able to sign up for a slot at the Activities Fair or online in just a few weeks, stay tuned!
